About Us

The Iman Jibril Charity Foundation was founded in 2022 by Mrs. Ikram Ahmed in honor of her children, Iman and Jibril. What began as a mother’s personal mission is now a continent-wide movement to restore hope, dignity, and opportunity for underserved communities.

From its roots in the Eastern Region of Ghana, the Foundation has grown into a force for change building schools, clinics, and programs that touch thousands of lives. Inspired by Ikram’s own journey from refugee child to global entrepreneur, our work is a living legacy of resilience and compassion.

We don’t do charity as usual. We build for generations creating sustainable solutions that uplift communities today and secure brighter futures tomorrow.

Join us in this mission. Together, we can transform lives, one community at a time.

All Round Community Development Programmes

By confronting today’s most urgent challenges, Iman Jibril Foundation employs a comprehensive, multifaceted strategy to strengthen communities in need.

This full-circle development programme is devoted to breaking the persistent cycle of hunger, poverty and unemployment throughout Africa and the world at large.

DRAG

Feeding Festival & Birthday Drive

On Iman’s 1st birthday, over 800 children in Asuboi were fed, given backpacks, supplies, and treated to haircuts and games; a celebration of joy and dignity.

Partnership for Change

Through our partnership with Qatar Charity Ghana and JBC Charity, we constructed a permanent facility offering education, healthcare, and spiritual care for the community.

Women Empowerment

In collaboration with local health organizations, we have provided free medical checkups, medicine, and health education to underserved communities.

Health Outreach

We organize regular health outreaches, providing free medical checkups, medicines, and health education to underserved communities.

Education

Our education programs provide mentorship, skills training, and leadership opportunities for young people to thrive.
